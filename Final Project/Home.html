Education can transform kids' futures

The Wisdom Whisperer invests in schools, teachers, and communities to bridge the global learning gap – because every child deserves the right to learn. We help children get ready for kindergarten and learn to read by third grade — a major indicator of future success. We’re especially focused on reaching vulnerable children in the Philippines where early learning resources are scarce. Globally, we ensure that no child’s learning stops because they are caught up in crisis. Your donation supports our work to keep children healthy, educated, and safe.
